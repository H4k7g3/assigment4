About club

Milan is an Italian professional football club based in Milan. Milan is one of the most successful clubs in the world and the most titled from Italy in terms of the number of trophies. In 1963, he became the first Italian club to win the European Cup. Milan also holds the record for most UEFA Super Cups won with Barcelona (5 titles). In addition, it is one of six European teams deserving the right to host the League Champions Cup, which is a family-time winning club. The last trophy won to date was the 2016 Italian Super Cup.

Interesting facts about our club

Milan is a very popular football club with a great history. The red-blacks have a huge number of trophies, they won the national championship many times, won the Italian Cup and were the first in the Champions League. Millions of spectators from all over the world are closely watching the game of their idols, Milan has many fans in our country. The text of the article contains all the most interesting information about this team that every football fan should know.

The Milan derby is one of the oldest matches in the history of world football. It all started in the first decade of the twentieth century, when a law was introduced in the Italian championship, as a result of which only local athletes began to play for AC Milan. All foreign athletes were forced to move to the new Internationale club. Thus, it was during this period of time that the famous derby was born. Another name for the confrontation "Della Madonnina" comes from the statue of the Virgin Mary, which can be seen on the famous "Cathedral of the Nativity".

The San Siro Stadium is the most capacious sports arena in Italy, as more than 80 thousand fans can be in the stands at the same time during a football match. This sports complex is really unique, home games are held here not only by Milan, but also by Inter. The stadium received its second name in honor of the famous Italian football player Giuseppe Meazza, who became a two-time world champion and managed to play in both Milan teams. Concerts of the world's best music performers are held annually at the San Siro, and a large football museum is also available to fans.
